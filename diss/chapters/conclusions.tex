The airflight simulation component can be separated from the pursuit-evasion problem space entirely and applied to different decision making processes. An example of this might be search-and-rescue, where we could redefine the state space of the MDP to track visited areas instead of opponent parameters and create rewards that drive movement towards unexplored locations. In this case the action space of the MDP is the same, but we could also utilise totally different decision processes such as neural networks.
